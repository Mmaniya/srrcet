DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

VISION

Our vision is to provide an up-to-date education and to offer flexible technical assistance which will allow our graduates to be competitive professionals.

MISSION

  • To provide an excellent education in Electronic and Communication-related fields.
  • Upon graduation, students will be able to perform research in all interested areas of study. Promoting a healthy teaching environment that emphasizes depth, originality and critical thinking.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering is one of the best technically equipped departments with adequate space, excellent Classrooms and Seminar Hall equipped with OHP/LCD projector, and state-of-the-art laboratories.

The Department offer in BE(ECE), affiliated to Anna University and appoved by AICTE, Chennai, the Department offers practical-oriented guidance through 10 ably qualified teaching staff, with M.E and Ph.D Qualifications, whose vision and concern for human values and the environment impact and impart the necessary practical skills to the young minds.

The department undertakes real-time projects for the final year students in U.G., with a view to making students serve society, and true to our motto "to live to learn, learn to serve".

Features :

  • Excellent lab facilities with 24x7 internet and WIFI CAMPUS.
  • Practical oriented training to students based on Anna university syllabus to meet the current IT trends.
  • Three Computer labs having enough systems with the latest configuration and Internet facility.
  • Hands-on training on the following: Mobile application development lab (Circuit Debugging and Application testing in real time).
  • DSP/VLSI lab (ubuntu-VMware)- Matlab,IAR embedded ,Xilinx and P-spice Open source software development. Circuits lab - Creating and innovative with syllabus and designing various circuits

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs))

Graduates of the Electronics and Communication Engineering Program will achieve:

  1. Acquisition of the necessary background in mathematical, scientific and engineering concepts needed to succeed in industry/higher education.
  2. Experiential learning in analyzing, designing, implementing and verifying Circuit-based solutions to real world problems aligned with industry expectations.
  3. A solid foundation in Communication and Electronics and instilling a keen interest to constantly update knowledge in this ever-emerging field.
  4. The faculty to analyze, design, implement and verify real world problems aligned with industry expectations.
  5. The needed soft skills like communication, team work, leadership qualities, professional and ethical values and an ability to apply the skills and knowledge acquired to address societal issues.
  6. The right academic environment, conducive for lifelong learning needed for a successful professional career.

COURSE OUTCOMES

  1. To enable graduates to pursue research, or have a successful career in academia or industries associated with Electronics and Communication Engineering, or as entrepreneurs.
  2. To provide students with strong foundational concepts and also advanced techniques and tools in order to enable them to build solutions or systems of varying complexity.
  3. To prepare students to critically analyze existing literature in an area of specialization and ethically develop innovative and research-oriented methodologies to solve the problems identified.

FACILITIES(LAB DETAILS)

  1. DSP LABORATORY: This laboratory is designed to provide basic knowledge of about MATLAB Perform various signaling operation and operating basic add, subtract and shifting operation an DSP processor.
  2. C PROGRAMMING LABORATORY: This laboratory is designed to provide Complete Knowledge of C Language. Students will beable to develop logics to help the create Programs and Applications in C.
  3. VLSI LABORATORY: This laboratory is designed to develop skills to design and analyze simple IC design to its application and also performing.
  4. CIRCUITS LABORATORY: This Laboratoryprovides an Introduction basic circuit lab to LIC and Digital systems lab. It contains an enough components, testing devices and power supplies.
  5. MPMC LABORATORY: This Laboratory provides about the basic study of definitions of data definitions and Data Manipulation Commands, Procedures & Functions, Triggers , Exception Handling and Design using ER modeling , Normalization and Implementation.
  6. COMPUTER NETWORKS LABORATORY: This Laboratory provides about the study and use of network Commands, Socket Programming , Implementation and analyze various network protocols, simulation tools.
  7. ADVANCE COMMUNICATION SYSTEM LAB: This Laboratoryprovides to design and implement Solutions Using Various Communication setup and Modulation but combining with optical techniques.
  8. COMMUNICATION SKILLS LABORATORY:This Laboratory provides about the study of basic English knowledge. Speaking and Listening skills and trained everyone to get fluently in English.
  9. ENGINEERING PRACTICES LAB: This Laboratory provides essential facilities to learn and Implement the basic electronic components and its operation .and also about how to solder a component on board.
