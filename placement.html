PLACEMENTS DETAILS

OBJECTIVES

The placement cell offers an array of career services that empower students to explore and achieve their career goals. Its objectives are:

  • To create awareness among students regarding career options and help them in identify their career objectives.
  • To empower the student with life long career decision-making skills.
  • To identify the suitable potential employers and to help them achieve their hiring goals.
  • To organise activities concerning career planning and development.
  • To act as the anchor for all placement-related activities and to give students the maximum number of placements.

    • PRE-PLACEMENT INTIATIVES

    The Employability Training Camp is a unique one of a kind value addition programme offered to our students from the Second year onwards. This camp, which is run on a residential mode is offered completely free of cost. It is a matter of pride that this programme is one of the premier events in the Institution and every student looks forward eagerly to be part of this event.

    An aptitude skills training on programme was held on 8th, 9th, and 10th March 2017 for the second year PG student of different department of Sri Raaja Raajan college.

    A workshop on career guidance was organised and students were exposed to the different dimension of group discussion and personal interviews. Trainers with rich experience gave tips to the students. They also organised MOCK Group discussion and HR interviews.
